Customer Support Availability

Chat agents are online from 6 a.m. to 10 p.m. PT on weekdays and 8 a.m. to 6 p.m. PT on weekends. Phone support is limited to critical order issues and can be reached at 1-800-555-0199 from 7 a.m. to 7 p.m. PT Monday through Friday. For after-hours escalations, leave a voicemail or open a priority ticket in the Help Center; responses are guaranteed within 4 hours. Social media DMs are monitored but not intended for order adjustments.
