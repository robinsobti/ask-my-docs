Domestic Returns Policy

Items purchased online can be returned within 45 days of delivery for a full refund to the original payment method. Products must be unworn, unwashed, and include all original tags. Prepaid labels are available for contiguous U.S. addresses; a $5 restocking fee applies to clearance items. Refunds are processed within 3 business days of the package arriving at our warehouse. To start the process, visit example.com/returns and enter your order number along with the zip code used at checkout.
