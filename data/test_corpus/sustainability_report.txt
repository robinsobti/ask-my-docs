Sustainability Highlights 2024

This year we reduced manufacturing emissions by 18% through expanded solar investments at our primary factories. Eighty-five percent of our apparel line now uses recycled or organically certified fibers. We eliminated single-use poly bags for domestic shipments, replacing them with curbside-recyclable sleeves. A new buy-back program refurbished 12,000 garments that would have otherwise entered landfills. Read the full report at example.com/sustainability.
